# 💄 Cosmetics Trends & Consumer Insights Dashboard

A comprehensive **Business Intelligence Dashboard** developed using **Tableau** to analyze cosmetics market trends, brand performance, pricing strategies, customer ratings, sales distribution, and consumer skin-type preferences. This project transforms raw cosmetic product data into meaningful business insights through interactive dashboards and data visualizations.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

The **Cosmetics Trends & Consumer Insights Dashboard** is designed to help businesses, analysts, and decision-makers understand key market trends in the cosmetics industry.

Using Tableau, this project provides an interactive dashboard that enables users to explore product performance, pricing patterns, customer satisfaction, and consumer preferences across multiple cosmetic brands.

The dashboard is built with a focus on delivering actionable insights through clean, interactive, and business-oriented visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze overall cosmetics market performance.
* Compare sales performance across different brands.
* Understand product pricing strategies.
* Identify premium and budget-friendly brands.
* Examine the relationship between product prices and customer ratings.
* Analyze consumer preferences by skin type.
* Support data-driven business decision making.

# 📊 Dashboard Overview

## 1️⃣ Executive Summary Dashboard

Provides a high-level overview of the cosmetics dataset through important business KPIs.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* 📦 Total Products: **120**
* 💰 Average Price: **₹564**
* ⭐ Average Rating: **4.45 / 5**
* 🧴 Total Skincare Sales: **36,906**

### Visualizations

* Product Category Distribution
* Skin Type Distribution

---

## 2️⃣ Sales Performance Analysis

This dashboard compares sales across leading cosmetic brands.

### Objectives

* Identify top-performing brands.
* Compare brand sales.
* Evaluate market competition.

### Visualization

* Horizontal Bar Chart

---

## 3️⃣ Pricing Analysis

Analyzes pricing strategies across cosmetic brands.

### Includes

* Average Price by Brand
* Top 10 Most Expensive Products

### Business Value

Helps understand premium and affordable product positioning.

---

## 4️⃣ Customer Rating Analysis

Examines customer satisfaction and pricing behavior.

### Includes

* Rating Distribution
* Price vs Average Rating

### Visualizations

* Histogram
* Scatter Plot

### Purpose

Understand whether higher-priced products receive better customer ratings.

---

# 📈 Key Business Insights

✔ Skincare products dominate the overall market with the highest sales.

✔ The average customer rating is **4.45/5**, indicating consistently high customer satisfaction.

✔ Premium-priced products generally receive higher customer ratings.

✔ Sales among leading brands remain highly competitive with minimal variation.

✔ **Colorbar** has the highest average product pricing.

✔ **L'Oréal** offers comparatively budget-friendly products.

✔ Consumer preferences across different skin types remain balanced.

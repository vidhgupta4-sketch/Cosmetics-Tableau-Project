# 📖 Overview

The **Cosmetics Trends and Consumer Insights Dashboard** is a Tableau-based Business Intelligence project that analyzes cosmetics industry data to uncover customer preferences, product performance, pricing trends, and brand competitiveness.

The dashboard enables users to interactively explore data, identify market trends, and support strategic decision-making using visually appealing charts and KPI dashboards.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze cosmetic products across multiple brands.
- Compare pricing strategies and product ratings.
- Study customer preferences based on skin type.
- Identify premium product trends.
- Evaluate overall brand performance.
- Present actionable insights using interactive dashboards.

---

# 💼 Business Problem

The cosmetics industry generates massive amounts of product and customer data. Without proper visualization, identifying customer behavior, pricing patterns, and market opportunities becomes challenging.

This project addresses these challenges by creating an interactive dashboard that converts raw data into meaningful business insights.
